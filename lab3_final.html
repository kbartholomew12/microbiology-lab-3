Positive vs. negative staining
Positive staining

Stain: Cationic (methylene blue, crystal violet, safranin)

What stains: The cells

Background: Clear

Heat-fix: Required

Best for: Shape & arrangement

Negative staining

Stain: Anionic (nigrosin)

What stains: Background

Background: Dark gray/black

Heat-fix: Never used

Best for: Accurate cell size
